# The Growth Table — Brand Guide

---

## 1. Program Identity

### Full Name
**The Growth Table: A Growth-Stage Founder Program**

### Tagline
*Real partnerships. Real revenue. Real scale.*

### Powered By Line
Powered by Bank of America

### Partner Credit Line (long form)
Endeavor Southeast × Atlanta Tech Village | Powered by Bank of America

### Elevator Pitch
The Growth Table is the premier platform connecting corporate decision-makers with the most promising growth-stage founders across the Southeast. A joint initiative of Endeavor Southeast and Atlanta Tech Village, powered by Bank of America, it is a curated, operator-first program designed to accelerate revenue, relationships, and regional ecosystem strength—not fundraising, not pitching, not inspiration theater.

### External Positioning Statement
*"Endeavor Southeast, in partnership with Atlanta Tech Village and powered by Bank of America, is building the premier platform connecting corporates with the most promising growth-stage founders across the Southeast—driving real partnerships, real revenue, and real scale."*

### Target Audience
- Growth-stage founders ($5M–$50M+ in revenue)
- Corporate leaders and enterprise decision-makers
- Select operators and ecosystem partners

### What This Is NOT
- Not a fundraising or pitch event
- Not an investor panel
- Not general startup programming

## 2. Partner Logos

Three logos appear together on all Growth Table materials. Each has a defined role and should never be altered, recolored, or stretched.

### Logo Hierarchy

| Priority | Partner | Role | File |
|----------|---------|------|------|
| 1 | Atlanta Tech Village | Host / Primary brand | `../Brand assets/ATV/Logo options/atv-logo-horizontal.png` |
| 2 | Endeavor Southeast | Co-presenting partner | `../endeavor_teal logo (1) (1).png` |
| 3 | Bank of America | Presenting sponsor ("Powered by") | `../bofa_lo4_rgb_proudsponsor_Digital.png` |

### Lock-Up Order
When displaying all three logos together:
```
[ATV logo]     [endeavor logo]     [Bank of America — Proud Sponsor]
   LEFT            CENTER                      RIGHT
```

On dark backgrounds: use the ATV white logo (`Copy of ATV Logo white.png`) and confirm Endeavor teal and BofA logos are legible.

### Clear Space
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "A" chevron in the ATV logo on all sides of each logo.
- Never place logos directly adjacent — always use a thin vertical divider rule (`#E5E5E5`, 1px) between them.

### Minimum Sizes
- ATV horizontal logo: 120px wide minimum (digital), 1 inch minimum (print)
- Endeavor logo: 80px wide minimum
- Bank of America logo: 100px wide minimum

### Background Rules
| Background | ATV Logo Version | Endeavor | BofA |
|------------|-----------------|----------|------|
| White `#FFFFFF` | Black horizontal | Teal | Full color |
| Light Gray `#F7F7F7` | Black horizontal | Teal | Full color |
| Dark Navy `#03152A` | White horizontal | Teal | Full color |
| Pumpkin `#FF6813` | White horizontal | White (if available) | White (if available) |

## 5. Tone of Voice

### Voice Pillars
- **Operator-first** — speak to builders who are scaling, not raising
- **Direct** — outcomes over inspiration; say what founders will actually get
- **Curated** — this is selective and intentional, not mass-market
- **Regional pride** — the Southeast is the point, not a footnote

### Do Say
- "growth-stage founders"
- "real access to customers, distribution, and partnerships"
- "scaling companies"
- "operator-led"
- "revenue, relationships, real-world scaling"
- "curated," "high-trust," "closed-door"
- "corporate access," "enterprise decision-makers"

### Don't Say
- "pitch," "raise," "deck," "cap table," "term sheet"
- "startup community" (too early-stage)
- "ecosystem event" (too generic)
- "networking event" (undersells the value)
- "inspiring speakers" (no inspiration-only speakers)

### Sample Copy Patterns
- **Event invite subject:** `The Growth Table | [Month]: [Theme] — You're Invited`
- **Description opener:** `A curated breakfast for growth-stage founders and enterprise decision-makers across the Southeast.`
- **Value prop:** `Real access to [Corporate Partner]. Real conversations. Real outcomes.`

## 8. Co-Brand Usage Rules

1. **ATV is always the primary brand.** It appears first, largest, or most prominently.
2. **Endeavor Southeast is always the co-presenting partner.** Use full name "Endeavor Southeast" in copy; use the teal wordmark logo visually.
3. **Bank of America is always the sponsor.** Use the "Proud Sponsor" logo lockup file. Always pair with "Powered by Bank of America" in text.
4. **Never combine all three logo colors into the color palette.** The Endeavor teal and BofA colors are logo-use only — do not pull them into backgrounds, buttons, or headlines.
5. **Always get final sponsor approval** before publishing any material that features the BofA logo.
